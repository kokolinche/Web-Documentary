STARS ALIGN

HOW IT ALL BEGUN

Long time ago, in a small village of Denmark, a boy was born. On the day he was born, the stars aligned in a special way and gifted him with the best present that he was able to use all his life - his talent.

Since a young boy, music was a big part of Asbjørn’s life. He grew up listening to famous musicians such as David Lynch and Leland Sklar, but his main inspiration was his own father, who is also a musician. Asbjørn learned from him what love for music is and picked up special skills which are required in order to become a great musician.

SMALL STEPS LEAD TO GREAT SUCCESS

His journey through the world of music was a long and diverse one. From a young age he learned to play different instruments and never had only one favourite. His desire to gain as many skills as possible helped him know how to handle a variety of instruments.

His main break through in his career as a young musician started during his studies at Aarhus Conservatory. Even then he didn’t stop playing only one instrument. He played double bass, synthesizer, drums, flute and he even started his career as a DJ by playing at different parties, however by the time he reached his master degree, he chose the keyboards as his main instrument.

TRAVELS, TRAVELS...

Being a young musician means being involved in a variety of projects, along with other young musicians. In this way, Asbjørn had a big list of projects, which ment playing in different groups and performing very often at concerts around Aarhus, but Danmark wasn’t the only country lucky to have him perform here.

Asbjørn was always interested in the culture of different countries, especially when it comes to music. He decided to go over places in order to learn about their music history.

Senegal (2016)

Going to Senegal was his first experience abroad. It was a great opportunity for him, as he learned about the traditional instruments, such as the Mbalax, which is a type of drum specific to his country. In the same time, he improved his musical skills by playing the double bass.

Palestine (2016)

He decided to expand his knowledge about the music in different countries, by going for a few weeks to Palestine in 2016. While being there, he studied Arabic music and all it’s different regional music types and genres.

Cuba (2017)

Cuba was the next place on his To-Do list. Asbjørn was fascinated by Cuban music, as it is considered one of the richest and most influential regional music in the world. While there, he played the Cuban drums and really fell in love with the culture of this country.

FOLLOW THE PATH

At this points, Asbjørn is doing his Master Degree at Aarhus Conservatory. He creates and plays classical and subconscious music. He performs very often with his groups in multiple concerts, while working at receptions and other events.

To check out Asbjørn’s art, follow him on social media and SoundCloud.
